I'm a multidisciplinary creative based in Orlando who loves to work across everything, from concept art to 3D modeling, traditional art, and full-stack development.

I love both art and science, and teaching myself how to code while growing my art skills opened a whole new world of possibilities. Every medium teaches me something that makes the others better.

When I'm not making things (which is basically never) I'm studying them. Film, science, technology, you name it. I've read 264 books so far and I'm not slowing down!
